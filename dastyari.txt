Dast-Yari organization information

Name:
Dast-Yari

Purpose:
Dast-Yari is a voluntary community-based organization created to support people in need in Afghanistan.

Who Dast-Yari helps:
Dast-Yari supports:
- families living in difficult financial situations
- people who are sick and cannot afford healthcare
- widows
- orphan children
- people affected by urgent or emergency situations

Membership:
To become a member, a person should:
- contact Dast-Yari
- accept the organization’s rules
- contribute at least 100 SEK per month
- understand that membership is voluntary

Membership activity:
- The minimum contribution is 100 SEK per month.
- If a member misses more than 3 months of contribution within one year, the membership can become inactive.

Values:
Dast-Yari is based on:
- humanity
- trust
- transparency
- responsibility
- helping people with respect and dignity

Transparency:
Dast-Yari aims to handle contributions responsibly and clearly, so members can trust how the support is used.
