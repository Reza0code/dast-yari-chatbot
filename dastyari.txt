Dast-Yari organization information

Dast-Yari is a voluntary organization helping people in Afghanistan.

The organization supports:
- families in need
- sick people who cannot afford healthcare
- widows
- orphan children
- emergency situations

Membership:
To become a member:
- contact Dast-Yari
- agree with the organization rules
- contribute at least 100 SEK per month
- membership is voluntary
- voluntary
- minimum contribution: 100 SEK monthly
- if a member misses more than 3 months in one year membership becomes inactive

Values:
- humanity
- trust
- transparency
